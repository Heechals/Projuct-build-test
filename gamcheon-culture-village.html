Gamcheon Culture Village

The 'Machu Picchu of Busan'

Gamcheon Culture Village (감천문화마을) is a vibrant and picturesque neighborhood located in Busan, South Korea. Often referred to as the 'Machu Picchu of Busan' or the 'Santorini of Korea', it is famous for its colorful terraced houses, steep alleys, and quirky murals.

A Village of Art

This charming village was once a slum, but through an art project initiated by the local government, it has been transformed into a hub for artists and tourists. Every corner offers a photo opportunity, with art installations, cafes, and stunning views of the city and sea.

Highlights

  • Colorful Houses: Wander through the uniquely painted houses.
  • Murals and Art Installations: Discover hidden art around every corner.
  • Panoramic Views: Enjoy breathtaking views of the village and Busan coastline.
