N Seoul Tower

Seoul's Iconic Landmark

N Seoul Tower, officially the YTN Seoul Tower and commonly known as Namsan Tower, is a communication and observation tower located on Namsan Mountain in central Seoul, South Korea. Built in 1971, it stands as a symbol of Seoul, offering stunning panoramic views of the city.

Experience the View

Standing at 236 meters (774 ft), it is the second highest point in Seoul, and it provides an unforgettable experience for visitors. The tower is renowned for its 'Locks of Love' where couples attach padlocks to the fence as a symbol of their eternal affection.

Highlights

  • Observation Decks: Enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views of Seoul.
  • Locks of Love: A popular spot for couples to declare their love.
  • Namsan Park: Explore the surrounding beautiful park with walking trails.
