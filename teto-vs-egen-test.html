About Teto-Egen Personality Types

What is Teto-Egen?

The Teto-Egen personality type is an internet meme that categorizes personality types based on traits associated with Testosterone (Teto) and Estrogen (Egen). It's a form of pseudoscience, similar to MBTI or blood type personality theories, meant for fun.

Key Concepts: The Four Types

The theory proposes a 'love food chain' where each type is drawn to another to complement their own traits: Egen-nyeo → Egen-nam → Teto-nyeo → Teto-nam → Egen-nyeo.

Teto-nam (Testosterone Male)

Characterized by strong leadership, drive, and a straightforward nature. They are assertive, action-oriented, and prefer directness in relationships.

Egen-nam (Estrogen Male)

Known for being sensitive, delicate, and rational. They value emotional connection and empathy, often taking a more passive role in relationships.

Teto-nyeo (Testosterone Female)

Lively, proactive, and independent. They have a strong mentality, an instinct-driven thought process, and are not afraid to take the lead in love and life.

Egen-nyeo (Estrogen Female)

Possess a gentle, quiet demeanor and value emotional connection and stability. They are highly empathetic and prefer to be led in relationships.

A Word of Caution

This test is not based on scientific evidence and should be enjoyed for entertainment purposes only. Personality is complex and cannot be definitively categorized by simple types. Think of this as a fun meme, not a scientific diagnosis.
