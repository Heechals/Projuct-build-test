Learn Hangeul: The Korean Alphabet

Hangeul, created by King Sejong the Great in 1443, is renowned for its scientific design and ease of learning. Unlike many other writing systems, Hangeul was intentionally designed to be accessible to everyone, fostering literacy across the nation.

Each character is formed to visually represent the shapes of the mouth and tongue when making the sound, making it incredibly intuitive. Learning Hangeul is your first step to unlocking the rich world of Korean culture, from reading K-pop lyrics to understanding Korean dramas and communicating with locals.
