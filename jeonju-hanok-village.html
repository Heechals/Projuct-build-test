Jeonju Hanok Village

A Walk Through Korea's Past

Jeonju Hanok Village (전주한옥마을) is a charming village located in the city of Jeonju, South Korea. It is home to over 700 traditional Korean 'hanok' houses, making it one of the largest and best-preserved hanok villages in the country.

Culture and Cuisine

The village is not only famous for its beautiful architecture but also for its rich cultural heritage and delectable local cuisine. Visitors can experience traditional Korean life, participate in cultural activities, and savor Jeonju's renowned bibimbap.

What to Experience

  • Traditional Hanok Houses: Wander through beautiful traditional Korean homes.
  • Cultural Experiences: Try on a Hanbok, participate in tea ceremonies, and traditional crafts.
  • Jeonju Bibimbap: Indulge in the famous local dish.
